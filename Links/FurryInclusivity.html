Furry Inclusivity

It is no secret that the furry community is essentially progressive leaning, with a higher percentage of LGBTGI+ people amongst it than in many other communities. Furries value respect and mutual love a lot, and in turn, it leads to them being generally accepting of any new individuals that would be discovering themselves as furry.

It is because of that that the furry community has become essentially linked with the queer communities that it is akin to, when it comes to its public perception
